Information about animal abuse

By reporting animal abuse you can help animals with getting in better life. Are you a witness of animal abuse or do you know someone who abuses their animals, please call 144. You can call 144 in case of:

  • Neglected animals
  • Abused animals
  • Hurt animal
  • Animals in acute need
  • When in doubt if an animal is in acute need

When calling 144 make sure you:

  • Know the exact location
  • Know what is going on

More information?

Take a look at the information page from the Rijksoverheid or www.politie.nl/144 144 is open 7 days per week, 24 hours a day. If 144 is not available call 0900-8844 or when in acute need 112.
